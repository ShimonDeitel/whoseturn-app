Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Whoseturn - Chore Wheel ("Whoseturn", "the app") is built to work entirely on your device. This policy explains, plainly, what that means.

What data the app stores

Whoseturn stores the wheels, participants, and spin history you create directly on your device, using local file storage. This data is never transmitted anywhere — not to us, not to any analytics service, not to any third party.

What we do not do

  • No account creation or sign-in.
  • No network requests of any kind from the app.
  • No iCloud or CloudKit sync.
  • No advertising, no ad tracking, no third-party analytics SDKs.
  • No collection of names, contacts, photos, or any personal information beyond what you manually type into the app for your own use (participant names for your household's wheels), which stays on your device.

Subscriptions

Whoseturn Pro is an auto-renewable subscription processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information — Apple handles billing according to its own privacy practices. You can view, manage, or cancel your subscription in your device's Settings app under your Apple ID.

Children's privacy

Whoseturn does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because the app collects no information at all beyond data you enter and keep on your own device.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same address with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@shimondeitel.com.
