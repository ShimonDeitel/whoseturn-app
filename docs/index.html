Whoseturn - Chore Wheel

The fair way to decide whose turn it is. Spin a real wheel — no more arguing about dishes, trash night, or who picks the movie.

Self-balancing fairness

The wheel remembers how long it's been since each person had a turn and quietly weights the odds toward whoever's most overdue — while never picking the same person twice in a row.

Any rotation, any size household

Dishes, trash, walking the dog, picking tonight's movie — create a wheel for anything your household needs to share fairly.

Private by design

Everything stays on your device. No account, no ads, no data ever leaves your phone.
