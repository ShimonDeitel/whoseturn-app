Whoseturn turns "whose turn is it?" into a satisfying spin instead of a household argument.

Add everyone who shares a chore or a decision, name a wheel for it — Dishes, Trash Night, Who Picks the Movie, Walk the Dog — and give it a spin. A pointer ticks past each name as the wheel slows down, then settles with a satisfying clunk on whoever's turn it is.

Under the hood, the wheel isn't just random: it remembers how long it's been since each person had a turn and quietly weights the odds toward whoever's most overdue, while never picking the same person twice in a row. Over time, everyone gets a fair share without anyone having to keep score.
